# 🔍 User Research

---

## 🎯 Objective
Understand user behavior, pain points, and motivation barriers in fitness and yoga habit formation.

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Users lose motivation after a few days  
- Lack of visual progress reduces engagement  
- Complex apps increase drop-off rate  
- Users prefer simple daily tracking systems  

---

## 👤 Personas
Created user personas based on beginner yoga practitioners and busy individuals trying to build habits.

---

## 📌 Pain Points Identified
- No clear progress tracking  
- Overloaded interfaces in fitness apps  
- Lack of daily motivation cues
